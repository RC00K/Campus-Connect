Campus Connect

Stay connected with your ministry with a seamless and fresh content experience.

Features

User Driven

All content is managed by the group administors, there is a varity of use cases that will help keep your ministry and your people connected.

Cross-Platform

Content is available on both iOS and Android as well as all compatible browsers. Mobile app will be coming in the future.

No Ads

Get rid of clutter and let your content be seen. There are absolutly no ads of any kind, except for the ones you make yourself by engaging with users.

About

Campus Connect provides a seamless way to connect with everyone in your ministry. Allowing you to update members and leaders with relevant distraction-free information. Allowing for engaged with all your communities in an age of media-driven communication. The goal and reason behind developing this application is to provide church and college ministries, to better engage with their members and take away any distractions that other social platforms bring.
